About GIRISH ENTERPRISE

GIRISH ENTERPRISE is a proprietory concern owned by Mr. Girish J. Vajani. the companyâ€™s main business is of Trading and of agency in Food, Flavour & Fragrance Raw Materials and Chemicals.
The main co. was established in 1925 in the name of M/S Jayantilal Ramji. this co. was started by his father Mr. Jayantilal R. Vajani & then Mr. Girish J.Vajani joined this co. as working partner in the year 1975.
In 1985 girish enterprise was formed to carry business of trading, manufacturing & indenting in perfumery business.
Mr. girish vajani has got experience of nearly 45 years in this field. he has worked 10 years as full fledge broker in m/s .jayantialal ramji co.. he has come across many manufacturers and traders from All over india.
